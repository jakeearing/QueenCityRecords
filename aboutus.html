About Us









The Queen City Records Story

All of us here at Queen City Records are extremely passionate about bringing high-quality sounding fidelity to our client's music.
We are all avid vinyl collectors and musicians who understand artist's needs when it comes to physical copies of their art.
We will work with the artist every step along the way to ensure they are satisfied and a high-quality product is made! No matter the pressing size.
